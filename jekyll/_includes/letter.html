Dear Fellow Chamber Members

Happy 2016!

I am very honored and humbled to have been afforded the opportunity to take on the role of Chairman of the Board of the Loudoun County Chamber of Commerce for 2015.

I am extremely proud of the Chamber’s larger accomplishments this year:

  • The Chamber’s Policy Committee, through its PolicyMaker Series and Loudoun BizVotes, successfully educated our members and many other Loudoun County residents, bringing clarity to the fog of campaign rhetoric.
  • Following a huge membership drive in 2014, a special emphasis on new member retention by our Membership Chairman, Mitch Sproul, has successfully kept our total membership value steady for the past two years.
  • In its first full year of existence, the Loudoun Chamber Foundation will make two series of grants to assist local nonprofits in their efforts that coincide with the Chamber’s Strategic Plan.

For the past few years, I have been working closely with our incoming 2016 Chairman, Greg Jay. He is a longtime resident and businessman in Loudoun County, and has become a good friend of mine. I am confident that the Chamber is now in great hands.

Thank you for all for Being a Part of It!, and thank you again for granting me the privilege of leading the Loudoun County Chamber of Commerce for the last year.
